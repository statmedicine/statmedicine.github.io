Key Information: - What surgical issue is identified? - Any imaging findings? - What is their neurology examination demonstrating? - Weakness: acute vs chronic, GCS, distribution (UMN vs LMN, dermatomal vs myotomal vs radicular) - Acute: known time of onset/last well, focality, collateral Hx, anticoagulation - Chronic: progression, fatigability, recent infection, respiratory involvement - What procedure was performed and how many days post-op if appropriate? - Previous surgery? Are they known to another surgeon? - Are they on anticoagulation? - Are there any drains/stomas and what is their output? - What is their fasting status? - What management has occurred so far for this patient (e.g. spinal precautions, fasting, etc.) Tips & Tricks: - Preemptively have outpatient letters & outpatient neuroimaging ready in patientâ€™s file or eMR
